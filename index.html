What is Android ?

  • Android is developed by a consortium of developers known as the Open Handset Alliance , with the main contributor and commercial marketer being Google
  • The first beta version of the Android SDK was released by Google in 2007 , whereas the first commercial version , Android 1.0 , was released in september 2008
